## Calorie-Saving Tips

- **Choose steamed rice over fried:** Save 140 calories by swapping Fried Rice for White Steamed Rice
- **Opt for Broccoli Beef:** At 150 calories, it's one of the lightest protein options
- **Skip the Chow Mein:** At 510 calories, it's one of the highest-calorie sides
- **Mixed Veggies as a side:** Only 80 calories and provides fiber
