## Lowest Calorie Side: Mixed Vegetables

Mixed Vegetables is by far the lowest calorie side option at Panda Express, containing only **80 calories** per serving.

**Mixed Vegetables Nutrition Facts:**
- Calories: 80
- Total Fat: 0.5g
- Saturated Fat: 0g
- Carbohydrates: 16g
- Dietary Fiber: 5g
- Sugars: 5g
- Protein: 4g
- Sodium: 540mg

This side provides the most fiber (5g) of any side option while keeping calories minimal.

## Highest Calorie Side: Fried Rice
