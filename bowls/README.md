# Panda Express Bowl Nutrition & Calories

Panda Express bowls combine a side with one or more entrees. This guide breaks down the nutrition for popular bowl combinations to help you build a balanced meal.

## Understanding Panda Express Bowls

A Panda Express bowl typically includes:
- **One side** (rice, noodles, or vegetables)
- **One entree** (chicken, beef, seafood, or vegetables)

The total nutrition depends on your specific combination.
